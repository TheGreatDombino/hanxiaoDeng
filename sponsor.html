Curtis Institute of Music | May 28–31, 2026 | Philadelphia, PA

Registration is now open for the 2026 Symposium for Women and Non-Binary Bassists, taking place May 28–31, 2026 at the Curtis Institute of Music in Philadelphia.


Now in its third year, the symposium brings together an outstanding faculty and a vibrant international community of bassists for four inspiring days of lessons, masterclasses, workshops, and performances.


Founded in 2024 by bassist Hanxiao Deng, the symposium welcomes women and non-binary double bassists of all levels from around the world, offering a supportive environment for artistic growth, mentorship, and collaboration.

Program Highlights

  • One-hour private lesson with a faculty member
  • Five masterclasses with symposium faculty, with 30 minutes of individual performance time per participant
  • Building a Project Based Career workshop with Nina Bernat
  • Bass Set-up Adjustments, Maintenance and Home Repair workshop with Helen McIntosh
  • Neck Geometry and Shape for Playability workshop with Anne Marie Runco
  • Technique Fundamentals & The Art of Teaching workshop with Tracy Rowell
  • Composition workshop with Kebra-Seyoun Charles
  • Special faculty recital
  • Curtis campus tour led by Curtis students
  • Lunches with faculty and fellow participants
  • Free bass repair corner hosted by sponsoring luthiers (limited on-site basic maintenance and minor repairs available)
